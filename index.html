Controls

Mouse:
  • Left-click and drag to pan
  • Right-click and drag to select zoom area
  • Double-click to zoom in
  • Mouse wheel to zoom in/out
Touch:
  • Pinch to zoom
  • Double-tap to zoom in
  • Drag to pan
